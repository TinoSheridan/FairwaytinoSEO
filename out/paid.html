Paid Advertising Strategy

Drive immediate traffic with targeted paid campaigns

Paid Advertising Overview

This paid advertising strategy is designed to complement the organic traffic strategies (SEO, content marketing, and social media) already developed for fairwaytino.com. By implementing targeted paid campaigns across multiple platforms, Tino Sheridan can accelerate lead generation, increase brand visibility, and drive qualified traffic to his mortgage loan officer website.

Advertising Goals

  • Generate qualified leads from potential clients
  • Increase brand awareness in the Macon market
  • Support content distribution to larger audiences
  • Retarget website visitors who haven't converted
  • Fill traffic gaps while organic strategies gain momentum

Target Audience Segments

Home Purchase Audience

  • First-time Homebuyers: Adults 25-40 researching mortgage options
  • Move-up Buyers: Growing families looking for larger homes
  • Relocation Buyers: Professionals moving to the Macon area
  • Investment Property Buyers: Individuals looking to purchase rental properties

Refinance Audience

  • Rate-and-Term Refinancers: Homeowners looking to lower their interest rate
  • Cash-Out Refinancers: Homeowners looking to access equity
  • Debt Consolidation Seekers: Homeowners with high-interest debt
  • FHA-to-Conventional Converters: Borrowers looking to remove mortgage insurance

Referral Partners

  • Real Estate Agents: Local agents who can refer clients
  • Financial Advisors: Professionals who advise clients on financial decisions
  • Home Builders: Companies constructing new homes in the area
  • Attorneys: Real estate and estate planning attorneys

Platform Strategy

Google Ads

Search Campaigns

Target audience: All segments actively searching for mortgage solutions

Keyword Categories:
  • Mortgage loan officer Macon GA
  • Home loans Macon Georgia
  • Mortgage pre-approval Macon
  • First-time homebuyer loans Georgia
  • Refinance mortgage Macon GA
  • Cash-out refinance Georgia
  • VA loans Macon GA
  • FHA loans Georgia
  • Best mortgage rates Macon
Campaign Structure:
  1. Purchase Loans Campaign
    • Ad Groups: First-time buyers, Conventional loans, VA loans, FHA loans
    • Landing Pages: Purchase-specific pages with relevant forms
  2. Refinance Campaign
    • Ad Groups: Rate-and-term refinance, Cash-out refinance, Debt consolidation
    • Landing Pages: Refinance-specific pages with calculators and forms
  3. Branded Campaign
    • Ad Groups: Tino Sheridan, Fairway Macon, Fairway Mortgage
    • Landing Pages: Homepage and about page
Best Practices:
  • Use ad extensions (callout, sitelink, call, location)
  • Implement negative keywords to prevent irrelevant clicks
  • Create responsive search ads with multiple headlines and descriptions
  • Use location targeting to focus on Macon and surrounding areas
  • Set up conversion tracking for form submissions and phone calls

Display Campaigns

Target audience: Awareness and retargeting for all segments

Campaign Types:
  1. Retargeting Campaign
    • Target website visitors who didn't convert
    • Show ads on websites within Google Display Network
  2. Contextual Targeting Campaign
    • Target websites related to real estate, home buying, and finance
    • Focus on local news sites and real estate listings
Ad Formats:
  • Responsive display ads with multiple images and text variations
  • Static banner ads in standard sizes
  • HTML5 interactive ads for higher engagement

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Target audience: Primary focus on home purchase and refinance audiences

Campaign Types:

  1. Lead Generation Campaigns
    • In-platform lead forms for mortgage pre-approval
    • Targeting based on life events (recently moved, engaged, new job)
    • Custom audiences based on income, home ownership, and interests
  2. Traffic Campaigns
    • Drive users to specific landing pages on fairwaytino.com
    • Promote blog content and mortgage calculators
    • Target by demographics, interests, and behaviors
  3. Retargeting Campaigns
    • Website visitors within last 30-90 days
    • Engagement with previous ads or social content
    • Video viewers who watched at least 50% of videos

Ad Formats:

  • Single image ads with strong calls-to-action
  • Carousel ads showcasing different loan programs
  • Video ads explaining mortgage processes
  • Stories ads for more immersive mobile experiences

Targeting Parameters:

  • Geographic: Macon, GA and surrounding areas (25-mile radius)
  • Demographics: Age 25-65, homeowners and renters, income $50,000+
  • Interests: Home buying, real estate, finance, home improvement
  • Behaviors: Likely to move, in-market for home, first-time homebuyer
  • Life Events: Recently moved, engaged, new job, growing family

LinkedIn Ads

Target audience: Primarily referral partners and high-income borrowers

Campaign Types:

  1. Sponsored Content
    • Promote thought leadership articles
    • Share market insights and industry news
    • Highlight success stories and testimonials
  2. Message Ads
    • Direct outreach to real estate professionals
    • Partnership opportunities with financial advisors
    • Personalized messages to high-value prospects
  3. Lead Generation Forms
    • Professional-focused mortgage consultations
    • Investment property financing information
    • Jumbo loan pre-qualification

Targeting Parameters:

  • Geographic: Macon, GA and surrounding areas
  • Job Titles: Real estate agents, financial advisors, attorneys
  • Industries: Real estate, financial services, legal
  • Company: Local real estate brokerages, law firms
  • Income: $100,000+ for direct borrower targeting

Zillow Mortgage Marketplace

Target audience: Active homebuyers using Zillow

Advertising Options:

  • Zillow Connect: Receive leads from borrowers seeking lenders
  • Featured lender placement in Macon, GA area
  • Co-marketing with partner real estate agents

Benefits:

  • High-intent leads actively searching for homes
  • Integration with Zillow's popular home search platform
  • Ability to showcase reviews and ratings

Local Publication Advertising

Target audience: Local community members in Macon, GA

Publication Options:

  • Macon Telegraph (digital ads)

  • Community newsletters

Ad Types:

ROI Calculation Framework

Key Metrics

  • Cost Per Click (CPC): Estimated $3-8 depending on platform
  • Click-Through Rate (CTR): Target 1-3% for search, 0.5-1% for display
  • Landing Page Conversion Rate: Target 5-10% for form submissions
  • Lead-to-Customer Conversion: Target 10-15% of qualified leads
  • Average Loan Value: $250,000 (estimate)
  • Average Commission: 1% of loan value (estimate)

Performance Targets

  • Cost Per Lead (CPL): $50-100
  • Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): $500-1,000
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): 300-500%

Landing Page Strategy

Dedicated Landing Pages

  • First-Time Homebuyer Landing Page

    Focus on education and guidance, highlight down payment assistance programs, include testimonials from first-time buyers

  • Refinance Calculator Landing Page

    Interactive calculator showing potential savings, clear explanation of refinance process, testimonials from successful refinance clients

  • VA Loan Landing Page

    Eligibility information and benefits, streamlined application process, military-focused testimonials

  • Local Market Landing Page

    Macon-specific mortgage information, local market trends and insights, community focus and local expertise

Landing Page Best Practices

  • Mobile-optimized design for all pages
  • Fast loading speed (under 3 seconds)
  • Clear, compelling headlines matching ad copy
  • Minimal form fields to reduce friction
  • Trust indicators (NMLS ID, reviews, certifications)
  • Single, focused call-to-action per page
  • No navigation menu to prevent distractions
  • Thank you page with clear next steps
