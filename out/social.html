Social Media Strategy

Build community and generate leads through strategic social presence

Social Media Strategy Overview

This social media strategy is designed to increase visibility, engagement, and lead generation for Tino Sheridan's mortgage loan officer business. By leveraging the right platforms, content types, and posting strategies, this plan will help drive targeted traffic to fairwaytino.com and establish Tino as a trusted mortgage expert in the Macon, GA area.

Social Media Goals

  • Increase brand awareness in Macon, GA area
  • Drive website traffic to fairwaytino.com
  • Generate mortgage leads from social followers
  • Establish Tino as a trusted mortgage expert
  • Nurture relationships with clients and partners
  • Support content marketing and SEO efforts

Target Audience Analysis

Primary Audiences

  • First-time Homebuyers: Millennials (25-40) researching the home buying process
  • Move-up Buyers: Growing families looking to upgrade to larger homes
  • Refinancers: Existing homeowners looking to lower rates or access equity
  • Real Estate Agents: Potential referral partners in the Macon area

Platform Demographics

  • Facebook: Broad age range (25-65+), good for targeting both younger buyers and refinancers
  • Instagram: Younger audience (25-44), visual platform ideal for showcasing properties
  • LinkedIn: Professional network (30-55), excellent for B2B relationships
  • YouTube: Wide age range, ideal for educational content and personal branding

Platform Strategy

Facebook

Primary Audience

First-time homebuyers, refinancers, local community

Content Strategy

  • Educational posts about mortgage processes
  • Local market updates and news
  • Client success stories and testimonials
  • Live Q&A sessions
  • Community involvement and local events
  • Mortgage calculators and tools

Posting Frequency

3-5 times per week

Best Practices

  • Use a mix of text, image, and video posts
  • Create and participate in local community groups
  • Respond promptly to comments and messages
  • Use Facebook Live for Q&A sessions
  • Leverage Facebook Events for homebuyer seminars

Instagram

Primary Audience

First-time homebuyers, younger move-up buyers

Content Strategy

  • Visual content showcasing local properties
  • Behind-the-scenes of the mortgage process
  • Client closing day celebrations
  • Mortgage tips and facts as carousel posts
  • Local area highlights and neighborhood features
  • Personal branding content

Posting Frequency

3-4 times per week

Best Practices

  • Use high-quality images and graphics
  • Leverage Instagram Stories for daily updates
  • Create saved highlights for different mortgage topics
  • Use location tags and local hashtags
  • Incorporate Reels for short educational content

LinkedIn

Primary Audience

Real estate agents, financial advisors, business professionals

Content Strategy

  • Industry insights and market trends
  • Professional achievements and milestones
  • Thought leadership articles
  • Company news and updates
  • Networking events and professional development
  • Success stories with a business focus

Posting Frequency

2-3 times per week

Best Practices

  • Focus on professional tone and business value
  • Share original articles using LinkedIn Publishing
  • Engage with local business community posts
  • Connect with real estate professionals and financial advisors
  • Join relevant groups for mortgage professionals and real estate

YouTube

Primary Audience

Research-oriented homebuyers and refinancers

Content Strategy

  • Mortgage process explainer videos
  • First-time homebuyer guides
  • Refinancing tutorials
  • Market update videos
  • Q&A sessions and interviews with real estate professionals
  • Virtual tours of local neighborhoods

Posting Frequency

2-4 videos per month

Best Practices

  • Keep videos concise (5-10 minutes)
  • Create playlists for different mortgage topics
  • Optimize video titles and descriptions for SEO
  • Include calls-to-action to visit website or contact
  • Cross-promote videos on other social platforms

Engagement Strategy

Community Building

  • Respond to all comments within 24 hours
  • Ask questions in posts to encourage comments
  • Create polls and surveys to boost engagement
  • Tag local businesses and partners when relevant
  • Participate in local community conversations

Relationship Nurturing

  • Share and comment on partner real estate agents' posts
  • Congratulate clients on home purchase anniversaries
  • Recognize and thank referral sources
  • Highlight local businesses and community events
  • Engage with local community groups and pages

Lead Generation

  • Include clear calls-to-action in posts
  • Direct message follow-up with engaged users
  • Create lead magnets specific to social media audiences
  • Use "Contact Me" call-to-action buttons on profiles
  • Promote free consultations and rate quotes
