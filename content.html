Content Marketing Strategy

Create valuable content that attracts and converts mortgage prospects

Content Marketing Overview

This content marketing strategy is designed to establish authority, support SEO efforts, and provide value to potential mortgage clients. By creating and distributing relevant, helpful content, fairwaytino.com will attract qualified traffic and convert visitors into leads.

Content Goals

  • Establish authority in mortgage lending
  • Educate potential clients about mortgage options
  • Support SEO with keyword-rich content
  • Generate leads through valuable resources
  • Nurture prospects through the buying journey

Target Audience Focus

First-time Homebuyers

Educational content focused on the mortgage process, loan options, and first-time buyer programs

Move-up Buyers

Content about leveraging equity, upgrading homes, and balancing buying and selling

Refinancers

Information about refinance options, rate comparisons, and equity access strategies

Referral Partners

Professional content about mortgage trends, co-marketing opportunities, and client success stories

Content Formats

Blog Articles

Regular blog posts (800-1500 words) targeting specific keywords and addressing common questions.

  • How-to guides for first-time homebuyers
  • Mortgage program comparisons
  • Local market updates and trends
  • Refinancing decision guides
  • Frequency: 2-4 posts per month

Comprehensive Guides

In-depth resources (2000+ words) that serve as definitive references on specific topics.

  • Complete First-Time Homebuyer's Guide
  • The Ultimate Refinancing Handbook
  • Macon, GA Neighborhood Guide
  • VA Loan Eligibility and Benefits
  • Frequency: Quarterly

Visual Content

Engaging visual assets that simplify complex mortgage concepts.

  • Mortgage process infographics
  • Loan comparison charts
  • Local market statistics visualizations
  • Timeline graphics for homebuying steps
  • Frequency: Monthly

Video Content

Video explanations and walkthroughs for visual learners.

  • Mortgage process explanations
  • Q&A sessions addressing common concerns
  • Local neighborhood tours
  • Client testimonials and success stories
  • Frequency: 2 videos per month

Interactive Tools

Calculators and assessment tools that provide personalized insights.

  • Mortgage payment calculator
  • Refinance savings calculator
  • Affordability assessment tool
  • Rent vs. buy comparison calculator
  • Development: Quarterly updates

Email Content

Targeted email sequences and newsletters to nurture leads.

  • First-time homebuyer email course
  • Refinance consideration sequence
  • Market update newsletters
  • Rate alert notifications
  • Frequency: Bi-weekly newsletters
